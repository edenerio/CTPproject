DATALYTICS PHOENIX
"Providing transformative insights from arising data."

Our Mission:

    Our data analytics firm has been tasked by a consortium of centibillionaires to use Census data to pinpoint key factors that are directly affecting the AGI (Adjusted Gross Income).

     They are entrusting us to use our data analytical skills and to familiarize ourselves with Census data to provide insight on the underlying and overarching aspects that contribute to the poverty issue at large. They are asking for a final deliverable that makes suggestions on how to allocate the 2 billion dollars that they intend to donate.

     Our firm was motivated to take on this arduous project because we firmly believe that the science of data can provide insights that can be a catalyst for positive change in modern society. This quest starts with taking on the scourge of poverty.
